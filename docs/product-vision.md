# Offer Copilot Product Vision v1.0

## 1. Product Overview

Offer Copilot is an AI-powered career assistant designed to help job seekers improve their job search experience.

The product uses AI technology to assist users with job description analysis, resume optimization, interview preparation, and job application management.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

Many job seekers face challenges during the career search process:

1. They don't know whether their skills match a job description.
2. They struggle to optimize their resumes for different positions.
3. They lack effective interview preparation and practice.
4. They have difficulty tracking multiple job applications and interview progress.

Offer Copilot aims to solve these problems by providing an intelligent AI assistant throughout the job search journey.

---

## 3. Target Users

### Primary Users

- University students preparing for their first job
- Job seekers looking for new opportunities
- Professionals planning career transitions

### User Characteristics

- Need personalized career guidance
- Want to improve application success rate
- Need efficient tools to manage job searching

---

## 4. Core Features (Initial Version)

### 4.1 Job Description Analysis

Users can upload or paste a job description.

AI analyzes:

- Required skills
- Key responsibilities
- Matching degree with user's background
- Missing skills or experience

---

### 4.2 Resume Optimization

Users provide their resume.

AI helps:

- Improve resume structure
- Highlight relevant experience
- Optimize keywords
- Adapt resume for specific positions

---

### 4.3 Interview Preparation

AI provides:

- Common interview questions
- Personalized answers suggestions
- Mock interview practice
- Feedback and improvement suggestions

---

### 4.4 Application Management

Users can track:

- Applied companies
- Job positions
- Interview stages
- Follow-up actions

---
